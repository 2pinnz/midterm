Why Bloom?

Founded by a Taiwanese couple from Taichung—the birthplace of boba—Bloom brings fresh, authentic tea to Newark. We brew with real tea leaves, not powders, for bold flavor, non-artificial sweeteners, and a true taste of Taiwan in every cup.
